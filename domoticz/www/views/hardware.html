You can retreive old month records by running SBFSpot/SMASpot with commandline parameter -v -amXX,
where XX is the amount of months you would like to retreive, for example:
./SBFSpot -v -am12 , this will retreive up to 2 years from now.

This process can take a while, when pressing the above button, please wait until the dashboard is shown again!.
